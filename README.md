Altar Thirst is a strategic defense game where you protect a mystical altar from waves of enemies spawning from four towers. Manage resources, produce villagers, and make grim decisions to collect enough blood sacrifices to fulfill the altar's demands before you're overwhelmed.

Strategic Defense: Protect your altar from enemies attacking from four directions

Sacrifice Mechanics: Sacrifice villagers to satisfy the altar's thirst (30 required to win)

Wave-based Gameplay: Defend against increasingly difficult enemy waves

Defend the Altar: Enemies will spawn from four towers and attack your central altar

Blood splashed from defeated enemies

Manage Villagers:

Create villagers to gather resources

Build townhouses to produce more villagers

Make Sacrifices: Sacrifice villagers at the altar to satisfy its thirst

Win Condition: Sacrifice 30 villagers to the altar while surviving enemy waves
